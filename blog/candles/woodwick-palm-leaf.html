WoodWick - Palm Leaf Candle

{candles, reviews}

03-21-21

overview

This is one of my favorite candles simply because it has a wooden wick. The smell fills the room well and isn't nauseating. It smells like pineapples and coconut and it's probably one of my longer lasting candles. This is the second candle I've ever owned, and it brings me back to sophomore year of high school when I would sit and study for my history class for hours on end. I enjoyed this candle a lot and I've been wanting to purchase another one with a wooden wick.

specs

I think this candle is 22 oz but I'm not sure, because they don't make ones with this exact container anymore. This candle has green wax and a tapered vessel with palm leaves on it. The wick is made out of wood.

rating  8/10

I rate this candle highly because I enjoyed the smell and I constantly lit this candle on school nights when I was staying up at night. It's been a while, so I don't remember the exact scent, but it smelled strongly of coconut and vanilla. The reason why it doesn't score any higher is because I felt that these scents weren't entirely unique and could be found elsewhere. The vessel got kind of hot near the middle because it tapered in, and I wasn't table to find a way to repurpose it. The wick burned very nicely with the sound of crackling wood, and the candle didn't tunnel at all. Overall, a very solid candle that I would buy again.
